Legacy of Trust

WE ARE GALILEA CRUISE

We have been growing as a sound houseboat group in Kerala since 2013. Our speciality is maintaining strong relationships with our guests through personalized service.

With vast experience in Alleppey and Kumarakom, we offer 1 to 4 bedroom well-furnished houseboats with an experienced crew dedicated to making your stay memorable.
