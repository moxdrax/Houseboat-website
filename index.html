As the morning mist lifts from the tranquil Alleppey backwaters, a new standard of luxury emerges. Galilea Houseboat is more than a vessel; it is a floating palace designed for those who seek the profound beauty of silence and the elegance of Kerala's heritage.

Experience a rhythmic dance with nature as you glide through emerald canals lined with swaying palms. Every journey is a bespoke masterpiece, curated with artisanal precision to ensure your time on the water is nothing short of transcendent.
