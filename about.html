THE JOURNEY

A Vision Carved in Teak

Since our inception in 2013, Galilea Houseboat has been a labor of love rooted in the rich traditions of the Kuttanad region. What began as a singular dream to redefine the backwater experience has evolved into a hallmark of luxury hospitality in Kerala.

Every vessel in our fleet is handcrafted using ancestral techniques, where seasoned teak and bamboo meet contemporary architectural elegance. We don't just provide tours; we preserve a way of life while ensuring our guests enjoy the finest modern comforts amidst the timeless serenity of the Vembanad Lake.

For over a decade, we have remained committed to the art of the 'slow voyage', inviting discerning travelers to reconnect with nature, heritage, and themselves.
